**PURPOSE**  
  
The Dashboard is the presentation layer of the platform.  
  
Its responsibility is to visualize trusted business information generated by internal business engines.  
  
The Dashboard never owns business logic.  
  
The Dashboard never performs financial calculations.  
  
The Dashboard never communicates directly with external APIs.  
  
⸻  
  
**DASHBOARD PHILOSOPHY**  
  
The Dashboard exists to answer business questions.  
  
Not to display random numbers.  
  
Every screen must reduce uncertainty.  
  
Every screen must help the business owner make better decisions.  
  
Every visual component must have measurable business value.  
  
⸻  
  
**CORE PRINCIPLES**  
  
The Dashboard shall:  
  
* Consume only approved business engines.  
* Never calculate KPIs.  
* Never duplicate formulas.  
* Never modify business records.  
* Support explainability.  
* Support drill-down.  
* Support bilingual operation.  
* Support responsive layouts.
